# MARVEL DEV OPS - RESHUB

## PROJECT-OVERVIEW
The Student Off-Campus Residence Review & Reporting System is a web-based platform designed to help Walter Sisulu University students make informed decisions when choosing off-campus accommodation. Currently, students rely on university representatives and SRC members who may have personal or financial connections to landlords, resulting in biased information. Students only discover problems with infrastructure, maintenance, landlord behaviour, location safety, and transport after moving in.
  
This platform will allow verified WSU students to share honest, structured reviews of   residences they have lived in, report maintenance issues, and access aggregated ratings from peers. By creating transparency and accountability, the system empowers students to choose housing based on peer experiences rather than promotional information.
