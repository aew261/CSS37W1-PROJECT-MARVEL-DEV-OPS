## Persona 1: Thabo (New Student Looking for Housing) 
-**Age:** 19 
-**Occupation:** First-year student at WSU (Mthatha Campus) 
-**Tech comfort:** High – uses smartphone daily 
- **Goal:** Find a safe, affordable off-campus residence within walking distance, with 
reliable maintenance. 
- **Pain points:** Doesn't trust university representatives; worried about hidden 
problems (bad landlord, water cuts). 

### Scenario: Thabo chooses a residence 
Thabo is admitted to WSU and needs to find accommodation before the semester 
starts. He hears about Res Hub from a friend. He opens the web app, registers with his 
Student email, and searches for residences near Mthatha Campus. He filters by 
distance (< 2 km) . He sees Maple House with a 4.2 rating and reads several reviews. 
One review mentions water cuts, but another says they fix it quickly. He also sees a 
maintenance report history that shows most issues are resolved within 2 days. 
Satisfied, he decides to book a room and later writes his own review after living there for 
a month.

## Persona 2: Lerato (Returning Student Comparing Options)

- **Age:** 21
- **Occupation:** Second‑year student at WSU (Komani Campus)
- **Tech comfort:** Medium – comfortable with apps but prefers simple, intuitive design
- **Goal:** Find a better residence for her second year after a bad experience in her first year.
- **Pain points:** Previously lived in a poorly maintained residence with an unresponsive landlord; wants to avoid repeating that mistake.

### Scenario: Lerato compares and shortlists residences

Lerato is preparing for her second year and wants to move to a better residence. She opens Res Hub and logs in using her WSU email. She searches for residences near Komani Campus and uses filters to show only those with a safety score of 4 stars or higher. She sees three promising options: Maple House, City View, and The Grove.

She clicks on each residence profile to read recent reviews, paying close attention to comments about maintenance and landlord responsiveness. She also checks the landlord rating for each property. For Maple House, she notices several reviews praising the landlord's quick response to issues. She clicks the heart icon to save Maple House to her Favorites list.

A few days later, Lerato logs back in and goes to her Favorites page to compare the shortlisted residences side‑by‑side. She decides to book a room at Maple House. After moving in, she writes a detailed review about her positive experience, helping future students make informed decisions.


## Persona 3: Olwethu (System Administrator) 
-**Age**: 30 
-**Occupation**: Platform Administrator 
-**Tech comfort:** High – experienced with dashboards, databases, and system monitoring 
tools 
-**Goal:** Ensure the platform is safe, reliable, and that reviews are authentic and 
respectful 
-**Pain points:** Managing inappropriate or misleading reviews; handling large volumes of 
user reports; maintaining system performance and uptime 

### Scenario: Olwethu monitors reviews and platform activity 
Olwethu logs into the admin dashboard at the start of her day to monitor activity on the 
platform. Since all residence listings are uploaded and managed by her, her main focus 
is on reviewing user-generated content. She checks recently posted reviews to ensure 
they follow community guidelines and do not contain misleading, offensive, or harmful 
information. 
She also investigates user-reported issues, such as spam reviews  and landlords, and takes appropriate action, including removing content or 
issuing warnings. In addition, she monitors system performance to ensure the platform 
runs smoothly without downtime.
